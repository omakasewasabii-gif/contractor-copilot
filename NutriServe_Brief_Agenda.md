# NutriServe Brief Demonstration Agenda
**EPISD RFP 26-027: Food & Nutrition Services Software Solution**

**Date:** Monday, April 13, 2026  
**Time:** 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM MT  
**Stakeholders:** Sandra Burciaga-Velez & EPISD Selection Committee  

---

## 1. Point of Sale System (10:00 AM – 10:30 AM)
*   **Federal Program Parity**: Support for SBP, NSLP, CACFP, ASSP, and SSO.
*   **Site Management**: Automated handling for CEP and Non-CEP site regulations.
*   **Student Financials**: Real-time low/negative balance tracking and automated notifications.
*   **Interface Versatility**: Full support for physical keyboard entry, touchscreen kiosks, and handheld scanners.
*   **Offline First Strategy**: Full transaction capability during network interruptions with instant background synchronization.
*   **Family Portals**: Integrated online meal application and payment systems.
*   **Hardware Compatibility**: Live demonstration of Zebra handheld readers and Verifone P400 PIN pads.

## 2. Back of House Functionality (10:30 AM – 11:15 AM)
*   **Menu & Nutrition Intelligence**: 
    *   Cycle Menu management, recipe development, and special dietary accommodations.
    *   Automated nutritional analysis and student-specific allergen tracking/lockouts.
*   **Parent Transparency**: Demonstration of the Parent Menu Portal with allergen alerts and UI filters.
*   **Production & Inventory**: 
    *   Automated daily food production records and vendor ordering integration.
    *   Perpetual inventory tracking (FIFO), waste monitoring, and delivery scheduling.
*   **Institutional Reporting**:
    *   Direct Certification and Verification process management.
    *   Identified Student Percentage (ISP) monitoring and monthly reimbursement claims.
    *   Financial logs: Cash journals, Participation data, and End-of-Year reporting.

## 3. Software & Data Management (11:15 AM – 11:35 AM)
*   **Infrastructure & Security**: 
    *   Database backup procedures and Disaster Recovery (DR) protocols.
    *   Role-Based Access Controls (RBAC) and SHA-256 data encryption for sensitive information.
*   **Ground Control Dashboard**: Site monitoring, data exchange requirement procedures, and administrative sync tools.

## 4. Training, Implementation, and Support (11:35 AM – 12:00 PM)
*   **EPISD Phased Rollout Plan**: Structured three-phase deployment across Central Office and all 69 campuses.
*   **Data Migration**: Validated migration path from legacy systems with data integrity checks.
*   **Comprehensive Support**: 
    *   Onsite and Virtual training session roadmap + Digital training guides.
    *   Dedicated Help Desk assistance and Regional Account Management commitment.
